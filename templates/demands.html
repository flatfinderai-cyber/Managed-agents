🚫 No Application Fees (Ontario)

Ontario banned upfront application fees in January 2022. Any landlord asking for one is breaking the law. Report to the Landlord and Tenant Board.

⚖️ The 3× Multiplier is Not Law

No jurisdiction legally requires tenants to earn 3× the rent. Landlords use this rule to screen — you have the right to negotiate or find agent-verified listings.
